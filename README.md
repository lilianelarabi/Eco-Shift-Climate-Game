**Eco-Shift** is an educational game built with the **Unity Engine** and **C#**, designed to translate complex climate science into an interactive experience.  
Rather than presenting facts in static charts, Eco-Shift places players in a **side-scrolling, story-driven world** where every decision nudges Earth‚Äôs future in a better ‚Äî or worse ‚Äî direction.

## üéÆ Why Eco-Shift?
Climate change is one of the most pressing challenges of our time, yet communicating its urgency to younger audiences often falls short. Reports are overwhelming, and statistics feel abstract.  

Eco-Shift tackles this gap by combining:
- **Scientific accuracy** ‚Üí Real datasets from the [U.S. Greenhouse Gas Center](https://www.epa.gov/ghgemissions).  
- **Accessible design** ‚Üí A pixel-art side scroller that feels familiar but carries weight.  
- **Decision-based storytelling** ‚Üí Players see the *immediate consequences* of their actions, not just numbers on a graph.  

Guided by atmospheric scientist **Dr. R√≥is√≠n Commane**, players travel through pivotal moments in climate history, learning how individual and collective choices ripple across generations.

## üß© Core Mechanics
- **Interactive Choice System** ‚Üí Every decision affects both visuals and outcomes.

- **Dynamic Feedback** ‚Üí Positive actions reinforce sustainability, while harmful ones produce visible degradation.
